Honey

It's a sweet syrup produced by bees.
Time: 3 ~ 4 days
Bee House can produce unique types of honey when it is in range of a blooming flower (within 5 tiles horizontally, 4 tiles vertically). Bee houses can be placed inside the Greenhouse, but will not produce honey.
250 Default - 150 g
Tulip - 160 g
Blue Jazz - 200 g
Summer Spangle - 280 g
Poppy - 380 g
Fairy Rose - 680 g
